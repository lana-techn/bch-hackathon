## Q&A Preparation

### Expected Questions:

**Q: How do you prevent spam tokens?**
> A: The 0.005 BCH launch fee ($1.50) prevents spam while staying accessible. Compare to Ethereum's $500+ gas fees.

**Q: What if no one buys my token?**
> A: The bonding curve guarantees liquidity. You can always sell back to the curve, though at a lower price if you're the only buyer.

**Q: How do you make money?**
> A: 1% trading fee on all buys/sells, split 50/50 between platform and token creator. Plus the 0.005 BCH launch fee.

**Q: Why Bitcoin Cash instead of Ethereum/Solana?**
> A: Three reasons:
> 1. 100x cheaper fees ($0.0001 vs $5-50)
> 2. CashTokens are native - no complex smart contracts needed
> 3. Instant confirmations (0-conf)

**Q: What about security/audits?**
> A: Smart contracts are open source and audited. Bonding curve math is simple and verifiable. No admin keys - fully decentralized.

**Q: Can tokens be listed on other exchanges?**
> A: Yes! After graduation, tokens are standard CashTokens that any DEX can list.
